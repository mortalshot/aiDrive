Management

Flexible structure

Flexible structure that allows you to adapt your work environment to your needs. The hierarchy of our system allows you to use up to 4 levels of nesting. Space, Folder, Project, Task. This number of levels allows you to create the correct hierarchy for any task.
Modes

View modes

You have all the possibilities of project management open to you, using all the main ways of setting tasks. Kanban, Gantt, List, Spreadsheet
Communication

Flexible structure
